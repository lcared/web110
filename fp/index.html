A Simple Idea.

The Food is Love Project started with a simple idea: get restaurants back to work by making food for people who need it. Days into our local COVID-19 outbreak, Egan Orion, Marina Gray, and Linda di Lello Morton put the plan into place and started getting food out to the community around Capitol Hill, Central District, and Madrona. Since then, another team through Build Lake City Together has started in Northgate and Lake City to serve vulnerable families in the north end. Volunteers who work with those living outside stepped up to deliver food to that especially vulnerable population. Now, weâ€™re preparing to launch in the south end. Every week, we serve over 1200 meals to people who need it. We hope to expand that with every passing week.
