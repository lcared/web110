Those we serve

Food Vulnerable Families: Through our contacts with Seattle Public Schools, we’ve identified families with children who need access to healthy, flavorful, and abundant food during the COVID-19 pandemic. We deliver food Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays and include school lesson packets and non-perishable food bags, in addition to our restaurants’ prepared meals.

People Living Outside: Those living in encampments have had their normal food sources disrupted during the COVID-19 pandemic. Working with homeless advocates who have worked in the camps, we do deliveries of prepared food and water three days a week, to make sure those who are the most vulnerable get the resources they need to survive the outbreak and don’t suffer from lack of food.
