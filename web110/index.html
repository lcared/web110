About Me

As the founder of Leadership Career Advisors (LCA), Sharon’s impressive career spans over 20 years. She obtained a Bachelor’s degree in Healthcare Administration from Texas State University and then went on to obtain a Master’s Degree in Career Counseling and Academic Advising from Sam Houston State University before completing Doctoral work in Organizational Communication at Texas AM University.

In 2007, Sharon began in the talent acquisition field as a Staffing Manager/Technical Recruiter at Robert Half Technology. In 2015, she joined Amazon as an AWS Leadership Recruiter. Sharon started Leadership Career Advisors LLC in 2019 as a means to provide consulting services to help companies and job candidates maximize their potential in a caring environment that fosters growth and empowerment.

What can Sharon and LCA do for you?

Career Advising, Job Search Support, Academic Advising, Talent Acquisition, Hiring Leader Support, Technical Program Management for HR Systems.

Care Consulting: Bring a care-based philosophy to the talent management life cycle with candidate and employee care.
