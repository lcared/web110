Social Media Optimization also known as SMO

Social media optimization (SMO) is the use of social media platforms to market products, services or events. SMO is similar to search engine optimization (SEO) because it is intended to increase interest in websites, except that it is optimizing brands on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and sharing sites (“Social Media Optimization”).

There is an industry that has sprung up around building social media plans to help connect with target audiences. A strong SMO will allow a company to strengthen their brand, generate leads for their business, get more visibility online, and connect with their audience (Jackson). SMO and SEO go hand in hand today because being ranked number one on google doesn’t help you if your audience is on Twitter instead. Using the two in combination will increase the effectiveness of a company’s overall marketing strategy and going viral on social media is a marker of successful SMO.

Jackson (2017) offers six tips to social media optimization. First, it is important to have a strategy with goals and objectives. Second, do keyword research. Third, Optimize your profile. Fourth, optimize your content. Fifth, optimize your posting schedule. Finally, track and improve with social media analytics.

When you are building a SMO strategy there are seven things that you can do to set up your strategy (Jackson). “(1) Ensure your social goals solve challenges. (2) Extend efforts throughout your organization. (3) Focus on networks that add value. (4) Create engaging content. (5) Identify business opportunities through social media. (6) Engage instead of ignore. (7) Track, improve and market your efforts” (Jackson).
