Audience, Issues, and Approach

Client's Name and Final Decision Maker My client's name is Linda, She is the Director of Food is Love Project, and she is the final decision maker.
Client's Business and Website Name The business name is Food is Love Project. It is a non-profit that supports food insecure families.
Redesign or Brand New Website This will be a brand new website.
Type of Website It will be an identity and marketing website.
Client's Goals My client hopes to gain a presence on the internet for her new non-profit in order to get new grants and partnerships.
Audience Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Food Insecure Families - These are the recipients of the services of the organization. This includes both families in the Seattle area and people living in encampments.
  • Contact information for the organization.
  • Needs to be able to quickly manuever through the site.
  • Submit a review of the services they receive.
  • They need a form to submit their interest in the program.
  • Services page that lists the offered services and also focuses on what makes Food is Love Project unique among competitors.
  • Page with a map of the service area.
  • Contact page with a phone number, email address, and contact form.
  • Contact form that can be used to submit a review of the services.
  • Contact form that can be used to gather their need for services.
Restaurants - Local Seattle restaurants that are willing to supply food at 5 dollars a meal or that have access to grants and can provide meals for free.
  • They need a form to submit their interest in the program. This will include their capacity for providing meals and the frequency that they can supply meals.
  • The company's contact information.
  • A list of the offered services.
  • Examples of the audience Food Is Love Project serves.
  • Contact page with a phone number, email address, and contact form.
  • Services page that lists all of the groups that are served.
  • Contact form to gather their interest and circumstances.
Philanthropic Sources - This can be individuals, corporations, or other non-profit organizations who are interested in supporting the mission of Food Is Love Project.
  • They need a form to submit their interest in the program.
  • The company's contact information.
  • A list of the offered services.
  • Examples of the audience Food Is Love Project serves.
  • They need a link to be able to provide a direct donation.
  • Contact page with a phone number, email address, and contact form.
  • Services page that lists all of the groups that are served.
  • Contact form to gather their interest and circumstances.
  • A button that takes them to the fiscal sponsors website to make a direct contribution.
General Public - These are interested individuals that are neither food insecure or restaurants, but that have the potential of becoming a philanthropic source.
  • They need a form to submit their interest in the program.
  • The company's contact information.
  • A list of the offered services.
  • Examples of the audience Food Is Love Project serves.
  • They need a link to be able to provide a direct donation.
  • Contact page with a phone number, email address, and contact form.
  • Services page that lists all of the groups that are served.
  • Contact form to gather their interest and circumstances.
  • A button that takes them to the fiscal sponsors website to make a direct contribution.
  • About page that describes the mission and vision of the organization.
